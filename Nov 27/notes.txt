Date and Time Formatting Codes
Year

    %Y: Four-digit year (e.g., 2024)
    %y: Two-digit year (e.g., 24 for 2024)

Month

    %m: Two-digit month (01 to 12)
    %B: Full month name (e.g., January)
    %b: Abbreviated month name (e.g., Jan)

Day of the Month

    %d: Two-digit day of the month (01 to 31)
    %j: Day of the year (001 to 366)

Weekday

    %A: Full weekday name (e.g., Monday)
    %a: Abbreviated weekday name (e.g., Mon)
    %w: Weekday as a decimal number (0 to 6, where Sunday is 0)

Hour (24-hour clock)

    %H: Two-digit hour (00 to 23)
    %k: Hour (1 to 24), right-aligned
    %I: Two-digit hour (01 to 12)
    %l: Hour (1 to 12), right-aligned

Minute

    %M: Two-digit minute (00 to 59)
    %i: Minute (0 to 59), right-aligned

Second

    %S: Two-digit second (00 to 59)
    %f: Microsecond (000000 to 999999)

AM/PM

    %p: AM or PM (e.g., AM or PM)

Timezone

    %Z: Timezone name (e.g., UTC, EST)
    %z: UTC offset in the form +HHMM or -HHMM (e.g., +0200, -0500)

Week Number

    %U: Week number of the year (00 to 53), Sunday as the first day of the week
    %W: Week number of the year (00 to 53), Monday as the first day of the week
    %c: Locale’s appropriate date and time representation
    %x: Locale’s appropriate date representation
    %X: Locale’s appropriate time representation
